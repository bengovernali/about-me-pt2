ABOUT ME

Early Life

Birth to Move

I was born on August 25th 1993 in Long Island. I have a younger brother and an older sister. We lived in a three family apartment in Queens, NY. My mom was a High School English teacher and my dad did freelance security system installation. Just before I turned 10, we moved to a small town called Somers in Northern Westchester.

After the Move

Somers was a much different place than Queens. We went from being surrounded by concrete to living with 7 acres of forest in our backyard. During my middle/high school years I took up guitar, paintball, hobby modeling and joined the Boy Scouts.

"Adult" Life

College

Shortly after I turned 18 I left to attend Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) to study Mechanical Engineering. Aside from cramming for tests and pulling all-nighters studying I managed to have a lot of fun.

Post-College

After I graduated it took me a little while to find my first job. I worked as a Test Engineer for Cyient.Inc. I was contracted to work for Pratt & Whitney, a jet engine manufacturer. I assisted in engine assembly, engine test, and inspected engines pre and post run. After doing this for a while I switched into a project management role in the same network. After a little over a year of that, and a decent amount of consideration, I decided to leave and become a web developer. These days I spend most of my free time going to concerts, playing video games, and lifting.
